ngx-dropdown-ease

This library supports single and multiple selection, style customisation, translation, and many other options. Dropdowns automatically adjust their opening position if there is not enough space for the items to be displayed. If you use built-in animations, they will be adjusted accordingly to ensure that the effect remains consistent.
