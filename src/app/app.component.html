ngx-dropdown-ease

This library supports data communication between the calling component and the dropdown itself. It also enables the opening of multiple dropdowns and provides a variety of customizable options, including both custom and ready-to-use animations, along with dropdown configuration settings. Get full control on the dropdowns and implement it in your application in no time!
